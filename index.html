About Me

My name is Nicole, and I am an aspiring QA (Software Testing) specialist.

My interest in testing developed from a desire to understand how digital products work, identify their weak points, and make systems more reliable and user-friendly. I have a strong analytical mindset, attention to detail, and the ability to see not only what works, but also potential risks.

Currently, I am actively learning the fundamentals of software testing, developing my technical and logical skills, and gaining experience working with requirements, documentation, and user scenarios.

This website is part of my professional journey. Here, I showcase my projects, track my progress, and highlight the skills I am building as I grow in the QA field.

My goal is to become a specialist who not only tests products, but also contributes to creating high-quality, stable, and user-friendly solutions.
